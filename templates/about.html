Rubber Goose Debugger
  • About (current)
This is a tool designed to help programmers debug problems.
It was made by Darian Bailey for CS 6460: Educational Technology at Georgia Tech.
It is based on Chen, W. K., Liu, C. H., & Huang, H. M. (2017). Software Debugging Patterns for Novice Programmers.
